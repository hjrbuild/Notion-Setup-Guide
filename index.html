Notion setup guide for iOS and Android

Step 1 — Download Notion
1

Open the App Store

Tap the blue App Store icon on your home screen.

2

Search for Notion

Tap the Search tab at the bottom, then type "Notion" in the search bar.

3

Download & install

Tap "Get" next to the Notion – Notes & Docs app, then confirm with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.

4

Sign in or create an account

Open Notion, then sign in with your email, Google, or Apple account. New to Notion? Tap "Sign up" to create a free account.

Step 2 — Add the Widget
5

Long-press your home screen

Hold any empty area on your home screen until the icons start jiggling.

6

Tap the "+" button

Tap the "+" icon in the top-left corner to open the widget gallery.

7

Find & add the Notion widget

Search for "Notion" in the widget gallery. Choose your preferred widget size (small, medium, or large), then tap "Add Widget."

8

Position & customize

Drag the widget to your preferred spot, then tap "Done." Long-press the widget to edit it and choose which Notion page it displays.

Tip: You can add multiple Notion widgets pointing to different pages — great for pinning your daily notes, tasks, or a favorites list.
Step 1 — Download Notion
1

Open Google Play Store

Tap the Play Store icon on your home screen or app drawer.

2

Search for Notion

Tap the search bar at the top and type "Notion." Look for the official Notion – Notes & Docs app.

3

Install the app

Tap "Install" and wait for the download to complete. The app installs automatically.

4

Sign in or create an account

Open Notion and sign in with your email or Google account. New users can tap "Sign up" to create a free account.

Step 2 — Add the Widget
5

Long-press an empty area

Hold any empty spot on your home screen until a menu or edit mode appears.

6

Tap "Widgets"

Select "Widgets" from the options that appear at the bottom of the screen.

7

Find the Notion widget

Scroll through the widget list or search for "Notion." Long-press the Notion widget to pick it up.

8

Drop & configure

Drag the widget to your desired home screen location and release. Tap the widget to select which Notion page it links to.

Note: Widget steps may vary slightly depending on your Android device manufacturer (Samsung, Pixel, OnePlus, etc.), but the general flow is the same across all Android phones.
