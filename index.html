Notion · Mobile Setup

Get Notion on your phone
& add it to your homescreen

A quick walkthrough for iOS and Android

Part 1 · Download
1
Open the App Store
Tap the blue App Store icon on your home screen or in your dock.
2
Search for Notion
Tap Search at the bottom of the screen, then type "Notion" in the search bar.
3
Download & install
Tap Get next to Notion – Notes & Docs, then confirm with Face ID, Touch ID, or your Apple ID password.
4
Sign in or create an account
Open Notion and sign in with your email, Google, or Apple account. New user? Tap Sign up to create a free account.
Part 2 · Widget
5
Long-press your home screen
Hold any empty area on your home screen until the icons start jiggling and the screen enters edit mode.
6
Tap the "+" button
Tap the + icon in the top-left corner to open the widget gallery.
7
Find & add the Notion widget
Search for "Notion" in the gallery. Choose your preferred size — small, medium, or large — then tap Add Widget.
8
Position & customize
Drag the widget to your preferred spot and tap Done. Long-press the widget later to edit which Notion page it displays.
Tip: You can add multiple Notion widgets pointing to different pages — great for pinning your daily notes, task list, or a favorites page.
Part 1 · Download
1
Open Google Play Store
Tap the Play Store icon on your home screen or in your app drawer.
2
Search for Notion
Tap the search bar at the top and type "Notion." Look for the official Notion – Notes & Docs app.
3
Install the app
Tap Install and wait for the download to complete. The app installs automatically.
4
Sign in or create an account
Open Notion and sign in with your email or Google account. New user? Tap Sign up to create a free account.
Part 2 · Widget
5
Long-press an empty area
Hold any empty spot on your home screen until a menu or home screen edit mode appears.
6
Tap "Widgets"
Select Widgets from the options that appear — usually at the bottom of the screen.
7
Find the Notion widget
Scroll through the widget list or search for "Notion." Long-press the widget to pick it up and drag it.
8
Drop & configure
Drag the widget to your desired home screen position and release. Tap it to choose which Notion page it links to.
Note: Widget steps may vary slightly depending on your device manufacturer (Samsung, Pixel, OnePlus, etc.), but the general flow is the same across all Android phones.
